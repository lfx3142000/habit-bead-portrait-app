# Habit Beads Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: TBD

Habit Beads is a local habit tracker. It is designed to work without an account, ads, analytics, subscriptions, or cloud backup.

## Information Stored

Habit Beads stores the information you enter in the app, including habit names, optional subtitles, bead counts, dates, colors, and display preferences.

## Where Data Is Stored

Your habit data is stored locally on your device. Habit Beads does not upload your habit data to a server.

## Data Sharing

Habit Beads does not sell, share, or transfer your habit data to third parties.

## Purchases

Habit Beads may offer a one-time Google Play purchase to unlock unlimited habits and custom colors. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. Habit Beads does not collect or store payment card information.

## Cloud Backup

Habit Beads disables Android cloud backup so your habit data is not silently copied through device backup services.
